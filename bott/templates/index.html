Orthopia

Orthopia combines the cultural and spiritual essence of Orthodox Tewahedo and Ethiopian traditions to foster community engagement through a Telegram-based application.

Explore Orthopia

Features

  • ✔ User System: Manage users with Telegram ID, name, and profile details.
  • ✔ Post System: Share posts on Telegram with timestamps and metadata.
  • ✔ Built with Django for scalability and Python Telegram Bot for seamless integration.

Future Enhancements

Orthopia aims to introduce:

  • • Moderation tools for content management.
  • • Localization in Amharic and other languages.
  • • Features like reactions, comments, and discussions.
